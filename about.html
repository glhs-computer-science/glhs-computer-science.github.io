How Things Work

The Green Level computer science club primarily focuses on the non-competitive aspect of coding, which mainly includes learning & sharpening skills in various coding languages and collabing on & uploading projects. Our goal for this year is to have members create their own semester or year long projects

If you are interested in learning more about the club, or want to join, sign up on AllTimely! If you have any questions, email Mr. Fox or one of our officers. Prior coding experience is not required!
